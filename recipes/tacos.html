Taco Recipe

Description

These classic Ground Beef tacos feature homemade taco meat loaded into freshly fried taco shells. Serve up with all your favorite toppings for an easy family meal that takes less than 30 minute!

Ingredients

  • Pepper, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, and cumin
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Oil
  • Butter
  • Corn tortillas
