Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Fast, easy ready in 15 minutes and my family loves it. That's my idea of the perfect weeknight dinner. This easy spaghetti recipe checks off all those boxes and it's comforting, hearty, and great for chilly winter nights

Ingredients

  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons garlic powder
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 (16 ounce) box spaghetti noodles
  • 1-1 1/2 lb hamburger meat
